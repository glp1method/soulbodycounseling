The Approach

What's underneath is where the work actually happens

You've probably tried managing it. Maybe you've gotten good at it. Integrative therapy means we go underneath the managing — the stories held in your body, the patterns running the show, the thing you haven't fully looked at yet.

Using EMDR, Gestalt therapy, and Motivational Interviewing, we build a path that's yours — not someone else's template.
