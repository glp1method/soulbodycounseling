The Approach

Healing that honors the full complexity of who you are

You are more than your diagnosis, your history, or the labels others have placed on you. Integrative therapy means we work with your whole self — the stories held in your body, the patterns shaping your mind, and the deeper truth you're moving toward.

Using evidence-based modalities like EMDR, Gestalt therapy, and Motivational Interviewing, we build a path that's yours — not a one-size-fits-all protocol.
